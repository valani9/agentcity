# Devil's Advocate Separator -- Citations

## Primary anchors

1. **Janis, I. L. (1972)** *Victims of Groupthink.* Houghton Mifflin.
   The original prescription: separate proposer from critic.

2. **Schwenk, C. R. (1990)** "Effects of devil's advocacy and
   dialectical inquiry on decision making." *Organizational
   Behavior and Human Decision Processes*, 47(1). Empirical
   validation of structured dissent as a decision-quality
   intervention.

## Supporting anchors

3. **Klein, G. (2007)** "Performing a project premortem." *HBR*,
   September 2007. Source for the pre_mortem_step intervention.

4. **Kahneman, D. (2011)** *Thinking, Fast and Slow.* FSG. Source
   for the rubber-stamping failure mode + self-evaluation
   limitations.

## How citations are surfaced

- Every `AttachedPlaybook` carries an `anchor_citation` field.
- The system prompt names Janis 1972 and Schwenk 1990 verbatim.
