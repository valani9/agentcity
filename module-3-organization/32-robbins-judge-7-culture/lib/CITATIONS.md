# Robbins/Judge 7-Characteristics -- Citations

## Primary anchor

1. **Robbins, S. P., & Judge, T. A. (2017)** *Organizational Behavior*
   (17th ed.). Pearson. The canonical formulation of culture as a set
   of seven independent characteristics.

## Supporting anchors

2. **O'Reilly, C. A., Chatman, J. A., & Caldwell, D. F. (1991)**
   *People and organizational culture: A profile comparison approach to
   assessing person-organization fit.* Academy of Management Journal,
   34(3), 487-516. Empirical basis for profile-comparison methodology.

3. **Schein, E. H. (1985, 2017)** *Organizational Culture and
   Leadership.* Pairs naturally with #31 Schein Iceberg as the
   layered-coherence counterpart.

## How citations are surfaced

- Every `AttachedPlaybook` carries an `anchor_citation` field.
- The system prompt names Robbins & Judge 2017 verbatim.
