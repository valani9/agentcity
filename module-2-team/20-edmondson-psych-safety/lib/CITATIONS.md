# Edmondson Psychological Safety -- Citations

## Primary anchors

1. **Edmondson, A. C. (1999)** "Psychological Safety and Learning
   Behavior in Work Teams." *Administrative Science Quarterly*, 44(2),
   350-383. Original construct + four observable behaviors used
   throughout.
2. **Edmondson, A. C. (2018)** *The Fearless Organization.*
   Wiley. Operational guidance + the "high-safety teams report more
   issues" insight.

## Supporting anchors

3. **Janis, I. L. (1972)** *Victims of Groupthink.* Houghton Mifflin.
   Source for the failure mode "artificial_consensus" and the dissent_round
   intervention.
4. **Wang, Q., et al. (2023)** "Cooperative LLM Agents: A Survey."
   *arXiv:2308.00352*. Translation layer from human-team safety to
   multi-agent systems; informs the boundary-spanning behavior.

## How citations are surfaced

- Every `AttachedPlaybook` carries an `anchor_citation` field.
- The system prompt names Edmondson 1999 + 2018 verbatim.
