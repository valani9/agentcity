# Span-of-Control / Centralization -- Citations

## Primary anchors

1. **Galbraith, J. R. (1995, 2014)** *Designing Organizations.*
   Jossey-Bass. The Star Model establishes how span-of-control and
   centralization compose into structural-load profiles.

2. **Mintzberg, H. (1983)** *Structure in Fives.* Prentice-Hall. The
   five structural configurations inform when wide vs narrow spans
   are appropriate.

## Supporting anchors

3. **Graicunas, V. A. (1937)** *Relationship in Organization.* Bulletin
   of the International Management Institute. The original mathematical
   treatment of span-of-control: as direct reports grow linearly, the
   number of relationships a supervisor must manage grows
   combinatorially.

4. **Urwick, L. F. (1956)** *The Manager's Span of Control.* Harvard
   Business Review. The classical empirical anchor for the ~7
   direct-reports rule.

## How citations are surfaced

- Every `AttachedPlaybook` carries an `anchor_citation` field.
- The system prompt names Galbraith and Mintzberg verbatim.
