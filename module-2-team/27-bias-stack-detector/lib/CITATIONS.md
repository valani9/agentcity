# Bias-Stack Detector -- Citations

## Primary anchors

1. **Tversky, A., & Kahneman, D. (1974)** "Judgment under
   uncertainty: Heuristics and biases." *Science*, 185(4157).
   Anchoring, availability, representativeness.

2. **Kahneman, D. (2011)** *Thinking, Fast and Slow.* FSG. Modern
   synthesis: System 1 / System 2 + overconfidence calibration.

3. **Nickerson, R. S. (1998)** "Confirmation bias: A ubiquitous
   phenomenon in many guises." *Review of General Psychology*,
   2(2). Source for confirmation-bias scoring.

4. **Staw, B. M. (1976)** "Knee-deep in the big muddy: A study
   of escalating commitment." *Organizational Behavior and Human
   Performance*, 16(1). Escalation-of-commitment scoring +
   retry-cap intervention.

## Supporting anchors

5. **Arkes, H. R., & Blumer, C. (1985)** "The psychology of
   sunk cost." *Organizational Behavior and Human Decision
   Processes*, 35(1). Sunk-cost failure mode.

6. **Popper, K. (1959)** *The Logic of Scientific Discovery.*
   Source for the disconfirmation-search intervention.

## How citations are surfaced

- Every `AttachedPlaybook` carries an `anchor_citation` field.
- The system prompt names Kahneman & Tversky verbatim.
