# Glaser Conversation Steering -- Citations

## Primary anchor

1. **Glaser, J. E. (2014)** *Conversational Intelligence: How Great
   Leaders Build Trust and Get Extraordinary Results.* Bibliomotion.
   Source for the cortisol/oxytocin axis, the three conversation levels
   (transactional / positional / transformational), and the trigger
   catalog (telling-vs-asking, blame, agency removal, etc.).

## Supporting anchors

2. **Lieberman, M. D. (2013)** *Social: Why Our Brains Are Wired to
   Connect.* Crown.
   Neurochemistry underpinning Glaser's claims.

3. **Stone, D., & Heen, S. (2014)** *Thanks for the Feedback.*
   Penguin. Pairs with Glaser for difficult-conversation work; the
   `vstack.stone_heen_feedback` triggers compose downstream when
   cortisol is feedback-driven.

## How citations are surfaced

- Every `AttachedPlaybook` carries an `anchor_citation` field.
- The system prompt names Glaser 2014 verbatim.
