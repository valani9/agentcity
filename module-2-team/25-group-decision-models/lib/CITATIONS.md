# Group Decision Models -- Citations

## Primary anchor

1. **Kaner, S. (2014)** *Facilitator's Guide to Participatory
   Decision-Making* (3rd ed.). Jossey-Bass. The five-method
   aggregation framework + practical guidance on matching method to
   stakes/reversibility/buy-in.

## Supporting anchors

2. **Janis, I. L. (1972)** *Victims of Groupthink.* Houghton Mifflin.
   Source for the dissent-recording requirement and the
   `agentcity.devils_advocate` upstream composition.

3. **Surowiecki, J. (2004)** *The Wisdom of Crowds.* Doubleday.
   Source for the fist-to-five rationale: degree-of-agreement
   surfaces hidden information lost in binary votes.

## How citations are surfaced

- Every `AttachedPlaybook` carries an `anchor_citation` field.
- The system prompt anchors the LLM in Kaner 2014 verbatim.
