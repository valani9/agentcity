# Org-Structure Matrix -- Citations

## Primary anchors

1. **Galbraith, J. R. (1995, 2014)** *Designing Organizations: An
   Executive Briefing on Strategy, Structure, and Process.* Jossey-Bass.
   The Star Model (Strategy, Structure, Processes, Rewards, People)
   anchors the structural-fit framing.

2. **Mintzberg, H. (1983)** *Structure in Fives: Designing Effective
   Organizations.* Prentice-Hall. Five structural configurations (simple,
   machine bureaucracy, professional bureaucracy, divisionalized form,
   adhocracy) inform the archetype taxonomy and the six independent
   dimensions.

## Supporting anchors

3. **Burns, T., & Stalker, G. M. (1961)** *The Management of Innovation.*
   Tavistock. Mechanistic vs organic structures map onto the
   formalization x centralization quadrants used here.

4. **Lawrence, P. R., & Lorsch, J. W. (1967)** *Organization and
   Environment.* Differentiation-integration framework: foundation for
   the departmentalization dimension.

## How citations are surfaced

- Every `AttachedPlaybook` carries an `anchor_citation` field.
- The system prompt names Galbraith and Mintzberg verbatim.
