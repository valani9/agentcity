# McAllister Trust Dimensions -- Citations

## Primary anchor

1. **McAllister, D. J. (1995)** "Affect- and Cognition-Based Trust as
   Foundations for Interpersonal Cooperation in Organizations."
   *Academy of Management Journal*, 38(1), 24-59.
   The cognitive/affective trust distinction and the operational
   measures used for both. Source for the dimension-scoring rubric
   and the trust-quality classifier.

## Supporting anchors

2. **Goleman, D. (1995)** *Emotional Intelligence.* Bantam Books.
   Source for the affective-trust failure modes
   (absent_emotional_acknowledgement, no_stakes_named, performative_apology)
   and the upstream composition with `agentcity.danva_emotion` /
   `agentcity.goleman_ei`.

3. **Frei, F. X., & Morriss, A. (2020)** "Begin with Trust." HBR.
   Pairs upstream of McAllister: their Trust Triangle's authenticity +
   empathy legs map onto McAllister's affective dimension; their logic
   leg maps onto cognitive.

## How citations are surfaced

- Every `AttachedPlaybook` carries an `anchor_citation`.
- The system prompt anchors the LLM in McAllister 1995 verbatim.
