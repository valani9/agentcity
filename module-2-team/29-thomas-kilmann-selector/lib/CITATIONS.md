# Thomas-Kilmann Selector -- Citations

## Primary anchor

1. **Thomas, K. W., & Kilmann, R. H. (1974)** *Thomas-Kilmann
   Conflict Mode Instrument.* Xicom. Five conflict-handling styles
   plotted on assertiveness x cooperativeness; the instrument is the
   most-used conflict-style framework in industry.

## Supporting anchors

2. **Sharma, M., et al. (2023)** "Towards Understanding Sycophancy
   in Language Models." *arXiv:2310.13548*. Pairs with the
   accommodating-in-all-contexts failure mode.

3. **Schwenk, C. R. (1990)** "Effects of devil's advocacy."
   *OBHDP*, 47(1). Composes upstream of TK -- structured dissent
   forces collaborating where competing would short-circuit.

## How citations are surfaced

- Every `AttachedPlaybook` carries an `anchor_citation` field.
- The system prompt names Thomas & Kilmann 1974 verbatim.
