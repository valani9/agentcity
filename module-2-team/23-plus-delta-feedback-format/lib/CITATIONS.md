# Plus/Delta Feedback Format -- Citations

## Primary anchors

1. **Joiner Associates (1990s)** Plus/Delta facilitator training
   materials. The original format definition.

2. **Brown, B. (2018)** *Dare to Lead.* Random House. Modern
   re-popularization of plus/delta as a leadership-feedback default.

## Supporting anchors

3. **Stone, D., & Heen, S. (2014)** *Thanks for the Feedback.*
   Penguin. Companion text: plus/delta works because it neutralizes
   the three feedback triggers (truth/relationship/identity).

4. **Doran, G. T. (1981)** "There's a S.M.A.R.T. way to write
   management's goals and objectives." *Management Review*, 70(11).
   The keep-doing and alternative fields lean on SMART goal
   specificity -- composes naturally with `agentcity.smart_goal`
   downstream.

## How citations are surfaced

- Every `AttachedPlaybook` carries an `anchor_citation` field.
- The system prompt anchors the LLM in the Joiner / Brown lineage.
