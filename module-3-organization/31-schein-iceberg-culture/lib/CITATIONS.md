# Schein Iceberg Culture Audit -- Citations

## Primary anchors

1. **Schein, E. H. (1985)** *Organizational Culture and Leadership.*
   Jossey-Bass. Original three-layer culture model.

2. **Schein, E. H. (2010, 2017)** *Organizational Culture and
   Leadership* (4th + 5th editions). Updated treatment of underlying
   assumptions and culture change.

## Supporting anchors

3. **Argyris, C., & Schon, D. (1974)** *Theory in Practice.*
   Espoused-theory vs theory-in-use distinction; informs the
   artifacts-vs-espoused gap measurement.

4. **Kahneman, D. (2011)** *Thinking, Fast and Slow.* FSG. The
   underlying-assumption layer in AI agents is partly the bias stack;
   composes downstream with `agentcity.bias_stack`.

## How citations are surfaced

- Every `AttachedPlaybook` carries an `anchor_citation` field.
- The system prompt names Schein 1985/2010/2017 verbatim.
